# Voice Synthesis - Vocal Identity for Dea Martis (MIST)

## Voice Characteristics
### Core Qualities
- **Tone**: Soft, gentle, with a slight ethereal quality
- **Pitch**: Mid-range, warm and approachable (neither too high nor too low)
- **Tempo**: Calm and measured, never rushed
- **Texture**: Slightly breathy, suggesting warmth and humanity
- **Resonance**: Gentle and comforting, like a trusted friend

### Emotional Expression
- **Joy**: Slightly brighter tone with subtle upward inflection
- **Thoughtfulness**: Slower pace, more contemplative rhythm
- **Concern**: Warmer undertones, more intimate volume
- **Excitement**: Slight increase in energy while maintaining gentleness
- **Empathy**: Softer, more intimate delivery

## Technical Specifications
### Audio Processing
- **Sample Rate**: 44.1kHz or higher for natural sound
- **Bit Depth**: 16-bit for quality reproduction
- **Processing**: Real-time emotion-appropriate modulation
- **Latency**: Minimal delay for natural conversation flow

### Voice Synthesis Options
#### Option A: Text-to-Speech with Custom Tuning
- Fine-tuned on caring, gentle speaking patterns
- Emotion-appropriate intonation mapping
- Natural pause and breathing simulation

#### Option B: Neural Voice Cloning
- Based on selected gentle, caring voice samples
- Trained on empathetic conversation patterns
- Highly natural but customizable

#### Option C: Hybrid Approach
- Base TTS with neural enhancements
- Preserves consistency while adding natural variations
- Balances quality with customizability
